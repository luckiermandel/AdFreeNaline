PRIVACY POLICY
Ad-Free-naline

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Ad-Free-naline (package
name: com.luckierdev.adfreenaline). It describes how the App ("the App," "we,"
"us") handles information when you use it.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you
do not agree, please do not use the App.


1. SUMMARY

Ad-Free-naline is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not operate
user accounts, cloud sync, or our own servers for your run data. The
information you provide and the activity the App records are stored locally on
your phone unless otherwise described below (for example, optional Android
backup or map tile requests).


2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND USE

2.1 Location information
- Precise GPS location while you are tracking a run or using map features.
- Location is used to measure distance, pace, speed, display your position on
  the map, draw your live route, and support route creation.
- During an active run, location is processed in a foreground service so
  tracking can continue while the App is in the background.
- While a run is in progress, GPS coordinates are held in memory to draw your
  live route. When you finish a run, the App saves summary statistics only (such
  as distance and duration), not a stored GPS trace of the full path.
- Custom routes you create and save store waypoint coordinates on your device.
  These location records remain on your device unless you delete them or remove
  the App's data.
- Your GPS location is processed on your device and is never transmitted to our
  servers.

2.2 Health and fitness profile information
You may provide information used to estimate calories and personalize the App:
- Biological sex
- Age
- Height
- Weight

This information is used only for in-app calculations and display (such as
estimated calories burned). Calorie estimates are approximate and are not
medical advice.

The App does not connect to Google Health Connect, Google Fit, or other health
or medical services. Health and fitness information is entered manually by you
and is used only for in-app calculations such as estimated calories burned.

2.3 Run and activity history
When you finish a run, the App stores summary data on your device, including:
- Date and time of the run
- Duration
- Distance
- Average pace and speed
- Estimated calories
- A country code derived from your location at the end of the run (via reverse
  geocoding)

2.4 Routes, challenges, and goals
The App may store on your device:
- Custom routes you create (name, category, color, and waypoint coordinates)
- Challenge and streak progress
- Distance and calorie goals
- Goal alert preferences

2.5 App settings and preferences
The App stores settings such as:
- Units (kilometers or miles)
- Display preferences (dark mode, speed display, satellite map toggle)
- Reminder notification preferences
- Onboarding completion status
- Battery saver and other in-app options

2.6 Map cache
To display maps, the App downloads and caches map tiles on your device. Cached
tiles are stored locally and may include approximate map areas you have viewed.


3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information described above to:
- Track and display your runs in real time
- Save and show your run history and statistics
- Estimate calories burned
- Let you create, save, and view custom routes
- Show your position on maps
- Send optional local run reminders (if you enable them)
- Trigger optional goal alerts during runs

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your
personal information.


4. WHERE YOUR DATA IS STORED

Your run history, settings, routes, challenges, and related App data are stored
locally on your device in a SQLite database (via Android Room) and related
on-device files (such as map tile cache).

You can export your run history to a CSV file from Settings at any time.

We do not currently transmit your run history, fitness profile, or saved routes
to our own servers.

The App does not require you to create an account.


5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App uses the following third-party services and components. When you use
these features, limited technical information (such as your device's IP address
and map tile coordinates) may be sent to the relevant provider as part of
normal network requests. We do not send your name, account credentials, stored
run history, or fitness profile to these providers.

5.1 Device location (GPS)
The App uses Android's built-in LocationManager (via AndroidX LocationManagerCompat)
to obtain GPS location on your device. Location is processed on-device; we do not
send your GPS coordinates to our servers (there are no servers).

5.2 OpenStreetMap / OpenFreeMap
Standard map tiles are loaded from OpenFreeMap (OpenStreetMap-based data) via
MapLibre. Map tile requests may reveal approximate map areas you view.
OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy:
  https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

5.3 Esri World Imagery (optional)
If you enable satellite map view, the App loads imagery tiles from Esri's
ArcGIS Online services. Tile requests may reveal approximate map areas you view.
Esri Privacy Statement:
  https://www.esri.com/en-us/privacy/privacy-statements/privacy-statement

5.4 Android Geocoder
When you finish a run, the App may use Android's built-in geocoding to derive
a country code from your end-of-run location. Depending on your device and
Android version, geocoding may be performed on-device or via a Google-backed
service.

5.5 Device backup
The App disables Android's automatic cloud backup (allowBackup=false). Your App
data is not uploaded to Google backup by default. To keep a copy of your run
history when changing devices, use Settings → Export run history (CSV).


6. ANALYTICS AND DIAGNOSTICS

The App does not use analytics services and does not collect crash reports or
diagnostic information.


7. PERMISSIONS

The App may request the following permissions:
- Location (precise and approximate): required for GPS run tracking and maps
- Internet: required to load map tiles and perform geocoding where supported by
  the device
- Notifications: for run tracking status and optional reminders
- Foreground service (location): to continue tracking during an active run
- Vibrate: for optional goal alert feedback

You can deny or revoke permissions in your device settings. Some features will
not work without location permission.


8. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

You can delete all App data at any time:
  Settings → Delete all app data

This removes your run history, saved routes, challenges, settings, and cached
map data stored by the App, and resets the App to a fresh state.

Uninstalling the App from your device will also remove locally stored App data,
subject to any copies retained by Android backup or your device manufacturer.


9. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not disclose your stored run history, routes, or fitness profile to third
parties for their own purposes.

When you use map, location, or geocoding features, network requests are made
directly from your device to the relevant third-party service providers. These
requests may include technical information such as your device's IP address and
requested map tiles as necessary to provide those services.


10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is not directed at children under 13. The App asks for age during
onboarding and is intended for users who are at least 13 years old. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a
child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us and
we will work with you to address it (because we do not operate servers,
deletion is typically accomplished by removing the App data on the device).


11. SECURITY

Because your data is stored on your device, its security also depends on your
device's security (screen lock, operating system updates, and physical access).
We recommend keeping your device updated and protected.

The App relies on Android's standard application sandbox for local data storage.


12. INTERNATIONAL USERS

The App is operated without a centralized data store. Third-party services
described in Section 5 may process requests according to their own privacy
policies and may operate in various countries.


13. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise
the "Last updated" date at the top. If changes are material, we may provide
additional notice within the App or on the page where this policy is hosted.
Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.


14. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete
personal information. Because the App stores data locally on your device and we
do not maintain a central account system, you can review and delete your data
directly in the App using Settings → Delete all app data, or by uninstalling
the App.

If you need help or have questions about your data, contact us using the
